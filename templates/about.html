About Us

We are a team of four UC Berkeley MICS students who developed Intelligence Hub as part of our Capstone project.

Our platform is a comprehensive, all-in-one solution that consolidates data from trusted sources such as VirusTotal, Cloudmersive, AbuseIPDB, and CriminalIP. This integration enables users to efficiently verify Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) across multiple threat intelligence databases in a single location, saving valuable time while enhancing confidence in threat detection.

What sets Intelligence Hub apart is its advanced functionality. Beyond basic lookups, our system offers robust log analysis capabilities. Users can upload log files, automatically extract IOCs, and receive detailed, actionable threat reports, streamlining the threat identification and response process.
